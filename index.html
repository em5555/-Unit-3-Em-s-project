Knock Knock Who's There?

You find yourself locked in a mysterious room with many doors...not knowing what to do, you reach out and grab..

You take a quick peek in the dimly lit room only to feel something pull your arm..what is happening?!?

The yellow flickering light immediately catches your attention, but more so, you see a long hallway with a door at the end...wait..what are those heavy footsteps?!?!

Another person?!?! Why are you both locked in this house? Then you realize that at the same time, you grabbed the door handle, they also grabbed it on the other side. This person says they plan to escape, but can you trust this unknown person?!?!?

The footsteps are getting louder and heavier and you have no option but to run. You're running and you feel someone chasing you. Aha, you manage to open the door! You see the outside!! But as soon as you take one step out the door, it's too late. A big broad arm reaches you, pulling you back in. The door shuts.
